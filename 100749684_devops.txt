Information about the COMP 3104 DevOps Course

In the COMP 3104 course, students will learn the essential procedures, protocols, 
and best practices of a DevOps engineer to enhance workplace productivity.

They will analyze and evaluate different DevOps implementations and engage in hands-on work using industry-standard tools.

The course also focuses on creating solutions to complex business problems by following core DevOps responsibilities.

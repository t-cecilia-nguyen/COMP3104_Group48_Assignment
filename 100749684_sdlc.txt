Importance of learning DevOps in the Software Developmenet Cycle

Learning DevOps is essential in the software development cycle as it enhances collaboration between development and operations teams,
leading to faster and more efficient software delivery.

It improves software quality through continuous feedback and automation, ensuring consistency and reducing errors.

Additionally, it promotes automation and standardization,
which not only saves time and costs but also ensures consistency across different environments.

DevOps enables teams to meet market demands more effectively by delivering high-quality software at a quicker pace.
